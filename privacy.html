Privacy Policy

ECOSYSTEM URL CAPTURE  ·  Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Ecosystem URL Capture Chrome extension ("the extension") collects, why, and how it is handled. The extension is a collaboration tool that lets authorized members file webpage URLs into shared, themed collections held in a hosted database.

1. What the extension is for

Single purpose: when you click the extension on a browser tab and choose one or more collections, the extension records that page's URL into those collections so that members of an ecosystem can aggregate startups and websites by theme. The extension does nothing in the background and does not act on tabs you do not explicitly capture.

2. Data we collect

We collect data only when you are signed in and only at the moment you click Capture:

  • Page URL of the active tab you choose to capture (this is "web history" — but only for pages you deliberately submit, never your browsing in general).
  • Page metadata you submit alongside it: the page title and the original (uncleaned) URL.
  • Your account identity: the email address you log in with, used to attribute each capture and to enforce which collections you may write to.
  • IP address of the request, recorded server-side at capture time.
  • Browser user agent string, recorded server-side at capture time.
  • Timestamp of the capture.

We do not collect your general browsing history, keystrokes, form contents, page contents, cookies, or any data from tabs you do not explicitly capture.

3. How data is stored

Captured data and account information are stored in a hosted Supabase (PostgreSQL) database operated for this project. Your login session token is stored locally in your browser via the Chrome storage API solely to keep you signed in; it is not transmitted to anyone other than the Supabase authentication service.

4. How data is used

  • To file the URLs you submit into the collections you select.
  • To attribute each capture to its contributor and show recent activity.
  • To enforce access control (which collections each member may read or write).
  • To deduplicate URLs and maintain data quality.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose unrelated to the collaborative collection feature described here.

5. Who can see your captures

Access is role-based. Captures in a collection are visible to administrators and to members granted access to that collection. Your contributor email is visible to administrators and is stored with each capture you make.

6. Permissions the extension requests

  • activeTab — to read the URL of the current tab when you click Capture.
  • storage — to keep your login session on your device.
  • host_permissions: https://*.supabase.co/* — to send captures to, and authenticate with, the Supabase backend.

7. Data retention & deletion

Captures persist until removed by an administrator. To request deletion of your account or of captures attributed to you, contact us at the address below and we will action the request.

8. Third parties

The only third-party processor is Supabase, which hosts the database and authentication service. No other third parties receive your data. Review Supabase's own privacy terms at supabase.com/privacy.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact
